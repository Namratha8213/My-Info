The best movies/series according to Namratha

My top 3 movies/series

Harry potter series

The series of books known as "Harry Potter " written by J.K. Rowling recounts the adventures of a wizard named Harry Potter in a magical school called Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Over the course of seven books Harry and his companions encounter obstacles, including a dark wizard named Voldemort. These stories explore themes such, as friendship, bravery and the eternal battle between good and evil. "Harry Potter" has left a lasting impact on our culture due to its captivating storytelling, world building and enduring popularity, across generations.

The man who knew infinity

"The Man Who Knew Infinity" is a moving film that tells the incredible story of Srinivasa Ramanujan and his partnership, with G.H. Hardy. Dev Patel and Jeremy Irons deliver performances capturing the essence of their characters. The movie beautifully delves into their bond navigating through the complexities of academia and cultural diversity.

Mary Kom

"Mary Kom" is a captivating sports drama that depicts the life story of Mary Kom, a boxer magnificently portrayed by Priyanka Chopra. The movie beautifully showcases her journey, from origins to reaching the pinnacle as a world champion. It is a testament, to her commitment unwavering resolve and unyielding spirit in the face of challenges.
